📜 Patch Notes

Version 2.0
The Architect's Rebirth
Release Date: 30/03/2026
✦ The Abyssal Forge: Uncontested Conduit control now triggers the Forge, expanding Rift size or spawning Elemental Cores (Ruby, Topaz, Emerald, Sapphire) for permanent unit buffs.
✦ The Void Snap: Highly charged Conduits that remain contested for 3 turns will now collapse, triggering a Void Snap that permanently deletes those squares from the map.
✦ Dynamic Ascension: Sacrificing a trapped unit at the Shrine now offers unique Ascension Paths (Path A or Path B) based specifically on that unit's role, such as Shaper, Brawler, or Siphoner.
✦ Veteran Promotion: Specialist units that vanquish 2 enemies achieve Veteran status, unlocking new permanent passives or secondary abilities like Glacial Fortress or Volatile Forge.
✦ Leader Ultimates: Leaders now channel their Ultimates to generate charges. Once active, they emit a persistent Aura, though the Leader remains locked down while channeling.
Version 1.5
The Specialist Update
Release Date: 12/11/2025
This major update overhauls the core tactical gameplay and AI. Standard units are replaced by a new roster of unique specialists, each bringing new active abilities and strategic roles to the battlefield.
✦ New Mechanic - Contested Conduit: The Conduit Link no longer breaks when an enemy steps on a rift. Instead, it becomes "Contested," reducing the Anchor power boost from +2 to +1 until the enemy is removed.
Version 1.4.1
Primal Reckoning
Release Date: 08/11/2025
This update introduces powerful new mechanics for leaders, factions, and the Shrine.
✦ Leader Ultimates: Leaders gain a one-time-use Ultimate: King's Edict (Frost Lord) and Tyrant's Proclamation (Ash Tyrant). A new visual flair shows when the ultimate is ready.
✦ Shrine Rework - The Primal Sacrifice: The Overloaded Shrine now "Traps" a unit. The owner can then choose to "Sacrifice" it (faction-wide +1 Power buff) or "Release" it (-1 perm power & Daze).
✦ New Mechanic - Steal the Sacrifice: While a unit is Trapped, an opponent can move onto the Shrine to "Steal" the sacrifice, claiming the buff for their team.
✦ New Synergies: Ignition Point (Mark of Cinder + Lava Glob) and Conduit of Frost (Chilling Aura + Glacial Wall) have been discovered.

Version 1.3
The Awakened Conduit
Release Date: 07/10/2025
A total overhaul reshapes the Mythic Territories! Version 1.3 is our largest update yet, introducing a deep strategic evolution to the rift system, a completely remastered arsenal of warriors, and a fully responsive interface.
✦ Total Visual Remaster: Every warrior on the battlefield has been reborn with stunning, all-new high-quality artwork. Command a visually reimagined army and immerse yourself in the conflict like never before!
✦ Play Anywhere Interface: The game now fully supports all screen sizes, from mobile phones to desktops. The entire user interface has been redesigned for a seamless experience, no matter how you play.
✦ New Mechanic - The Awakened Conduit: The rifts are no longer passive zones of chance. By occupying both rifts, players can now form a Conduit Link, unlocking incredible new strategies:
  • Conduit Highway: Teleport friendly units across the map between the two linked rifts.
  • Empowered Anchors: The two pieces holding the rifts gain +2 Power and unique defensive or tactical abilities.
