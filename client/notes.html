VERSION 1.4.1

Primal Reckoning

calendar_today 08/11/2025

This update introduces powerful new mechanics for leaders, factions, and the Shrine.

✦

Leader Ultimates: Leaders gain a one-time-use Ultimate: King's Edict (Frost Lord). A new visual flair shows when the ultimate is ready.

✦

Shrine Rework - The Primal Sacrifice: The Overloaded Shrine now "Traps" a unit. The owner can then choose to "Sacrifice" it (faction-wide +1 Power buff) or "Release" it (-1 perm power & Daze).

✦

Steal the Sacrifice: While a unit is Trapped, an opponent can move onto the Shrine to "Steal" the sacrifice, claiming the buff for their team.

✦

New Synergies: Ignition Point (Mark of Cinder + Lava Glob) and Conduit of Frost (Chilling Aura + Glacial Wall) have been discovered.
