Game Guidebook

Master the elemental forces of Snow and Ash. A comprehensive guide to survival, conquest, and ascension in the Mythic Territories.

flagThe Objective

Command the elemental forces of Snow or Ash. The primary victory condition is simple yet daunting: Vanquish the enemy leader. Capture the enemy's Frost Lord or Ash Tyrant to secure your dominion.

swordsCore Gameplay

Turns & Movement

Only one unit may be moved per turn. A full round consists of a Snow turn followed by an Ash turn. Most units move 1 square orthogonally or diagonally, unless restricted by Hamstrung or Stuck status effects.

Combat & Territory

Move onto an enemy unit's square to initiate combat. The unit with the higher Effective Power wins and captures the enemy. In the event of a tie, the player who controls more territory wins. Moving into an empty square automatically converts it to your faction's territory (crucial for tie-breakers and ability mechanics).

Abilities, status effects, and special terrain modifiers (such as Unstable Ground, Icy Ground, and Void Scars) directly affect Effective Power. Many specialist and legendary effects interact with combat resolution — see the Unit Profiles section below for details.

Two power rifts located in opposite corners can link to activate the Conduit, enabling anchors, teleports, charges, and Abyssal Forge events.

The Conduit becomes active when one faction controls both Rift areas. While linked, anchors receive a power boost, and friendly units nearby can utilize the Conduit highway to instantly teleport between anchors.

The Abyssal Forge periodically expands the Rift area or spawns valuable Elemental Cores. Under heavy charges, the Forge may trigger a Void Snap, leaving behind permanent Void Scars.

temple_hindu The Unstable Shrine

Capturing the Shrine grants a permanent +1 Power upgrade and adds a charge to the Shrine. Upon reaching 2 charges, the Shrine overloads, trapping the next unit that enters it.

View Sacrifices & Releases expand_more

The owner of a trapped unit must choose between two outcomes:

  • Primal Ascension: Sacrifice the unit for a permanent, powerful faction-wide upgrade.
  • Release: Free the unit with -1 permanent Power and Dazed for 3 turns.

Be cautious: opponents can steal your trapped sacrifice by moving their own units onto the Shrine.

Primal Ascension Paths

Sacrificing a unit grants a permanent, powerful faction-wide upgrade. Each unit category offers a choice between two paths when Ascension is activated. Below are the three primary paths:

architecture

Shaper (Ice Weaver / Rift Forger)

Path A: [Master of Creation]

Leader gains a one-time wall or hazard placement ability.

Path B: [Territorial Claim]

Vanquishing an enemy unit automatically captures two adjacent empty squares.

shield

Priest (Soul Freeze / Scorch Priest)

Path A: [Leader's Ward]

Leader gains a one-time Priest's Ward that blocks the next enemy ability targeting them.

Path B: [Martyrdom]

When one of your units is vanquished, the attacking enemy becomes Dazed for 2 turns.

bolt

Striker (Frostbite Stalker / Cinder Scout)

Path A: [Lethal Precision]

Skirmisher unit permanently gains +1 Base Power.

Path B: [Targeted Weakness]

Friendly units gain +1 Power when attacking a debuffed enemy.
