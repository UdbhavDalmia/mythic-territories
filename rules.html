Game Guidebook

1. The Objective

Welcome to Mythic Territories, a game of strategy and conquest. Your goal is to achieve victory in one of two ways:

  • Vanquish a Rival: In "Pass and Play," you must outmaneuver a human opponent and capture their leader: the Ash Tyrant or Frost Lord.
  • Defy a God: In "Challenge the Architect," you must face the maker of the world itself. Prove that your mortal will can triumph over the flawless, primordial intelligence that forged the realms.

2. Core Gameplay

  • Turns: Each player moves one piece per turn. A full round consists of both players taking a turn.
  • Movement: Pieces may move one square in any direction, including diagonals.
  • Combat: Moving onto a square occupied by an enemy initiates combat. The piece with the higher Effective Power wins. If powers are equal, the player controlling more Territory wins.
  • Territory: Moving a piece to an unoccupied square converts it to your territory. This is the tie-breaker in combat.

3. The Battlefield

The board itself contains volatile and strategic locations that can turn the tide of war.

The Awakened Conduit (Rifts)

  • Forming the Link: When you occupy both rift zones simultaneously, a Conduit Link is formed. The two occupying pieces become Anchors.
  • Conduit Highway: While the link is active, any friendly piece adjacent to one Anchor can use its turn to teleport to an empty square adjacent to the other Anchor.
  • Anchor Bonuses: Both Anchors gain +2 Power. The top-left Anchor is immune to enemy abilities (Defensive Ward), while the bottom-right Anchor gains a one-time Rift Pulse ability, which pushes away all adjacent pieces and dazes them for one turn.

The Unstable Shrine

  • Charging: Capturing a piece on a Shrine square grants the attacker a one-time +1 permanent Power boost and adds one charge to the Shrine.
  • Overload: After three charges, the Shrine becomes Overloaded. The next piece to enter is vaporized by the raw energy! This also unleashes a Cataclysmic Pulse: all pieces adjacent to the shrine are pushed back one square and become Dazed for 2 turns.

4. Special Abilities

Many pieces possess unique abilities that consume their turn to use. These abilities have cooldowns, meaning they cannot be used for a set number of turns after activation.

Siphoner Abilities (Void Chanter & Rift Warden)

The Void Chanter and Rift Warden share the unique Siphon ability.

  • Siphon: Use a turn on a Rift or Shrine to gain 1 Charge (max 3). These charges can be spent to unleash powerful, faction-specific abilities detailed below.

Snow Faction Unleash

  • (1) Flash Freeze: Freezes an enemy piece within 4 squares, preventing it from moving for 2 rounds.
  • (2) Glacial Step: Teleport to any friendly territory square *within 5 squares*.
  • (3) Whiteout: Reduces the power of all enemies within a 3-square radius by 1 for 1 round.

Ash Faction Unleash

  • (1) Stoke the Flames: Grants a friendly piece within 4 squares +2 Power for 3 rounds.
  • (2) Rift Assault: Teleport to an empty square adjacent to an enemy, up to 3 squares away. The user becomes Dazed for their next turn and cannot move.
  • (3) Burning Ground: Creates damaging terrain in all adjacent squares that lasts for 1 round. Enemies entering take 1 permanent damage.

Piece-Specific Abilities

  • Chilling Aura (Soul Freeze): Activates an aura that reduces the power of all adjacent enemies by 1. The aura lasts for 3 turns. (Cooldown: 4 turns)
  • MarkOfCinder (Scorch Priest): Marks an enemy within 2 squares, reducing its power by 1 for 3 turns. (Cooldown: 4 turns)
  • GlacialWall (Ice Weaver): Creates two impassable walls on adjacent empty squares. The walls last for 3 turns. (Cooldown: 6 turns)
  • Unstable Ground (Rift Forger): Makes an empty square within 4 squares hazardous for 3 turns. The next piece to enter takes 1 permanent damage. (Cooldown: 4 turns)
  • SummonIceWisp (Cryomancer): Summons a Power 0 wisp to an empty square within 4 squares. This wisp can be used for blocking or capturing territory. (Cooldown: 4 turns)
  • Lava Glob (Magma Spitter): Deals 1 permanent damage to an enemy with a *base power* of 1 or 2, within a 4-square range. (Boosts do not affect targeting). (Cooldown: 10 turns)

Inherent Abilities

These abilities are passive and trigger automatically under certain conditions.

  • Territory Surge (Yeti & Hell Hound): When this piece captures an enemy, you immediately gain control of 2 random, unoccupied squares on the board.
